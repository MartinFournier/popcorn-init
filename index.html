What is popcorn-init?

Popcorn initiative is a neat solution to the usual boring initiative rolls during a standard Pathfinder combat.
It is an original idea by @TheAngryDM

For details about what it is and what it does, make sure to read his blog post.

This tool was made to help myself during our campaigns, but feel free to use it. I'll throw in my twitter, in case, you know.
